Revolution

Ghinwa Moujaes
Apr 20, 2020

On Women & Revolutions

Let the woman in you roar
Because for too long, she’s been silenced
Let the woman in you fight
Because they expected you to always be frightened
Let the woman in you scream
Because your country needs you to fight today
Let the woman in you glow
Because without you, this revolution is so grey

Rise from the ashes because
For too long, too much has been taken from you
Rise from the ashes and see
This land is for me & for you too
Rise from the ashes and say
Unity, unity what a beautiful view
Rise from the ashes and see
Your hopes will never again be so few

This revolution is female
And our rightful future we will declare
This revolution is inclusive
For too long, they’ve made us question our own flair
This revolution is fearless
And our leadership, they will have to endure
This revolution is endless
That, my determination can assure
