Seven

Ghinwa Moujaes
Apr 25, 2020

On love & numbers

7 can only be divided by 7 and by 1
7 can only be controlled by 7 and by 1
7 has a heart
7’s heart can only be touched by 7 and by 1
Every number from 0 to 7
And from 7 to infinity
Has a heart
Which can be touched
By a different one
I think we all know who 1 is
I have a 1
You have a 1
You probably don’t have a 1
But you’re looking for a 1
1 is the number that no matter
How many numbers you add to the
List of numbers you know,
That 1 will always stand out
1 is the number that no matter how much
The distance increases between you and 1
Something will always pull you back to that 1
7 has dreams
7 hopes to some day become as big as 77
Or maybe one day increase exponentially
To a number beyond the understanding of mathematical definitions
To infinity
But even 77
Divided by 11
Will go back to 7
And 7 only needs 1
And 7 only loves 1
And although 7 can be divided by 1
1 does not make 7 small or not whole
1 does not change 7
But rather
1 allows 7 to remain 7
But a better version of 7
And in a world where being prime
Has become quite odd
